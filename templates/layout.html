Oony

We help you shop smarter

2010-2016

Oony is an online and mobile platform that helps people shop smarter, from the everyday grocery shopping to their dream vacation. Every day we gather thousands of coupon codes and great deals from stores around the world to make really simple for our users to find the best opportunities. The business model is based on publishing products from our partners to drive them more sales. We create high-quality leads by intelligently matching consumers to the products they like. We feature more than 3 million deals in 15 countries.

Highlights

  • Founded in 2011 » Profitable in 2012
  • Raised a seed round from Enzyme Venture Capital
  • Part of NXTP Labs Accelerator
  • 1 million registered users
  • 15 countries
  • + 3 million active deals & products
  • + 150 million featured deals up to date

Weegoh

Your Social Nearby

2008-2010

Weegoh was the first location-based social network for mobile devices in Latin America. We proposed a new way of using your mobile around venues and events to discover the best places to go and meet new people. It's main competitor in the US is "Foursquare". We pivoted this project into Oony with the same goal of helping people discover great product and services from local business but with a different approach.
